Katakana & Hiragana

  1. Start with Syllables.txt v0.1
  2. Filter with ascii2ipa.pl to produce a new Syllables.txt
  3. Filter new Syllables.txt with katahira.pl
  4. Final results are the the two tables:
    • Katakana mapped onto IPA
    • Hiragana mapped onto IPA

To produce the Hiragana table a trivial edit must be made to the katahira.pl file. Please verify the tables and correct the conversion scripts as needed. To view the tables I have used the IE5.5 with the TITUS Cyberbit font and the "User Defined" encoding system set.
